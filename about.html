About Zomato

Your go-to platform for discovering and ordering food from the best restaurants around you.

Our Mission

Zomato aims to bring the best dining experiences to people by providing an easy-to-use platform for food discovery and delivery. Our mission is to ensure that no one ever has a bad meal.

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Zomato started as a restaurant discovery platform. Over the years, it has evolved into a complete food ecosystem, offering services such as food delivery, table reservations, and even grocery delivery in some regions.

Key Features

  • Search and discover restaurants in your city.
  • Read and write reviews about restaurants.
  • Order food online and get it delivered to your doorstep.
  • Reserve tables at top restaurants.
  • Subscribe to Zomato Pro for exclusive dining benefits.

Global Presence

Zomato operates in over 25 countries, helping millions of users explore, order, and enjoy food. Our platform connects customers with restaurants in major cities across the globe.
